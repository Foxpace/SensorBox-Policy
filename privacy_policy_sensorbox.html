Measurement process - sensor data, location and other

The "data" meaning in this section - all csv files, which consists sensor data (accelerometer, gyroscope), location data (from GPS, cellular networks and other) gathered by smartphone / smartwatch by SensorBox

  • Which data can be accessed by SensorBox ?

    • Sensor Data
    SensorBox can access internal sensors and their outputs like accelerometer, gyroscope and other, which are installed in your smartphone by vendor.

    Location Data
    In order to save GPS coordinates on your phone/smartwatch in form of csv, location services have to be enabled in the background. Data are accessed only with permission and consciousness of the user.

    List of features with usage of location:

    • Save coordinates to csv file
    • Show your current location on map (only in foreground)
  • When are the data accessed ?

    • The data are accessed only during measurement. The measurement can be started only by the user by picking specific sensors to store in the device locally. The measurement is manifested by notification or by UI in the SensorBox, which is designed to show measurement. The SensorBox cannot start measurement on its own.

  • Where are data stored?

    • The data are stored locally in your device's memory, where the measurement took place.

  • Are data shared or sold to third parties?

    • No. The data are stored in local memory of the device, where the measurement took place. None of the data are shared with third parties or are uploaded to some kind of server. Data are stored only for personal use of the user of the SensorBox.
